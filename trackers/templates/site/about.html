The Site Tracker is a web app that allows us to track cosmic anomalies, signatures, and wormholes in wormhole space.
It was designed in early October of 2013 for the alliance Ocularis Inferno, and has since undergone many large revisions to become what it is today.

Though I am the original and main author of this and the other apps in the project, I would like to thank the following people:
Elroy and Tsal, for accepting and promoting this app, and for offering many excellent suggestions about how it should work,
Sumeko, for giving me a huge list of extremely detailed thoughts with explanations and suggestions,
Aes'lka Talosh, Judas, Marcus, James, and Judaires for offering suggestions, and
raylu and pakesson for making code contributions.
